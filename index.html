Pulmonology

Our services include treatments for common respiratory ailments such as Asthma, COPD, Tuberculosis (TB), pneumonia, lung cancers, birth defects of lungs, sleep disorders, snoring etc.

Rheumatologists

Our Rheumatologists deal mainly with clinical problems involving joints, soft tissues, certain autoimmune diseases, and the allied conditions of connective tissues. Essentially, they treat disorders that affect the musculoskeletal system.

Ophthalmology

Our aim is to provide comprehensive eye care to the people. Many systemic diseases like Diabetes Mellitus, autoimmune disorders can affect the eye and in such cases, our expert ophthalmologists shall work with the concerned specialists with the aim of delivering improved outcome to the patients.
